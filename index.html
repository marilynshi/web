What is a ccc?

A chocolate chip cookie is a type of cookie that has a plain cookie base with chocolate chips or chocolate morsels as its distinguishing ingredient.

More information at:

The New Yorker

Attributes of the perfect ccc

In my opinion, the perfect chocolate chip cookie is crispy in the edges, chewy in the middle, and soft in the center, with metled chocolate (preferably dark chocolate) throughout.
